What we do

Find out what a Primary Care Network (PCN) is, and what it does for your community.

Comprehensive Primary Care Services

We provide a full range of primary care services, including preventive care, acute illness and injury care, chronic disease management, and more. Our providers work with patients to develop personalized care plans that address their unique needs.

Preventive Care

We believe in the importance of preventive care to maintain optimal health and prevent future health problems. We offer routine check-ups, immunizations, screenings, and other preventive services to help our patients stay healthy.

Chronic Disease Management

Our providers have expertise in managing chronic conditions, such as diabetes, hypertension, and heart disease. We work with patients to develop personalized care plans to manage their conditions and improve their quality of life.

Behavioural Health Services

We understand the importance of addressing the whole person, including their mental and emotional well-being. We offer behavioural health services, including counselling and medication management, to support our patients' mental and emotional health.

Care Coordination and Management

We work closely with patients to coordinate their care across multiple healthcare providers and settings. Our care coordination and management services help reduce healthcare costs and improve patient outcomes.

Patient Education and Resources

We believe in empowering our patients to take an active role in their health.
