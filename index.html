Welcome to Washwood Heath Primary Care Network (PCN)

Our PCN represents a collection of nine GP Practices and hence representing approximately 55000 patients in the Washwood Heath Area of Birmingham. We work collaboratively with NHS bodies, Local Authority, other local PCNs, Neighbourhood Networks, community and charitable organisations for the support and well-being of our local population.
